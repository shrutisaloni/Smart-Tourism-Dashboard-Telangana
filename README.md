# 🗺️ Smart Tourism Dashboard — Telangana

> An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing domestic and foreign tourist trends across Telangana (2016–2019) to empower data-driven decisions for the state tourism department.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is part of the **Codebasics Resume Project Challenge** using real-world open data from the **[Open Data Telangana](https://data.telangana.gov.in/)** portal.

The goal is to help the **Telangana Government Tourism Department** understand visitor patterns, identify high-growth and underperforming districts, uncover seasonal trends, and forecast future tourism revenue — all through a single, executive-level Power BI dashboard.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze **domestic and foreign tourist** arrivals from 2016 to 2019
- Identify **top and bottom performing districts** by visitor volume and CAGR
- Discover **seasonal patterns** to plan tourism campaigns effectively
- Forecast **future tourist arrivals** and potential revenue contributions
- Provide **actionable recommendations** to improve tourism infrastructure and strategy

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| 🏙️ **District-wise Analysis** | Visitor breakdown across all Telangana districts |
| 📈 **Trend Analysis** | Year-over-year growth in domestic & foreign tourists |
| 📅 **Seasonality Insights** | Peak and off-peak months visualization |
| 📉 **CAGR Metrics** | Compound Annual Growth Rate by district |
| 🔮 **Forecasting** | Power BI native forecasting for future visitor projections |
| 💰 **Revenue Estimation** | Estimated revenue impact from tourist inflows |

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- **Hyderabad** dominates visitor traffic but several districts show strong emerging CAGR
- **Q4 (October–December)** is consistently the peak tourism season, aligned with major festivals
- **Foreign tourist growth** is concentrated in a small number of districts, highlighting diversification opportunities
- Several districts remain severely underpromoted with high potential for eco/heritage tourism
- Projections suggest strong double-digit growth in tourism revenue with targeted infrastructure investments

---

## 💡 Recommendations

1. **Invest in underperforming districts** with high natural/heritage potential (e.g., Mulugu, Bhadradri Kothagudem)
2. **Launch off-season campaigns** to flatten the seasonal spike and spread revenue across the year
3. **Adopt a "Dubai Model"** for MICE (Meetings, Incentives, Conferences, Exhibitions) tourism in Hyderabad
4. **Improve digital visibility** of lesser-known districts through government-backed promotion
5. **Develop tourist-friendly infrastructure** (hotels, transport, signage) in tier-2 districts
